Paris

Paris, the capital of France, is a global center for art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Its 19th-century cityscape is crisscrossed by wide boulevards and the River Seine.

Activities:

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower
  • Explore the Louvre Museum
  • Take a boat tour on the Seine River

Budapest

Budapest, Hungary's capital, is divided by the Danube River. Its Chain Bridge connects Buda and Pest, and the city is renowned for stunning architecture and thermal baths.

Activities:

  • Relax in the Szechenyi Thermal Baths
  • Walk across the Chain Bridge
  • Tour the Hungarian Parliament Building
